Process of practise

At the beginning of their stay at the CINEL Training Center, the students were introduced for their trainers. They were presented with the scope of activities and the manner of organizing trainings at the host institution. They got to know the laboratories in which trainings on electronics, information technology, automation, telecommunications and other professions are organized. Then students divided into groups began classes with their Portuguese trainers. The practice began with the presentation of the rules and regulations in force at the lab and health and safety training. A program of practise and methods for its implementation were also presented.

Students continued their practise for the next three weeks. In addition, in their free time they learned the basics of the Portuguese language, they visited interesting places of Lisbon, got to know the culture and everyday life of the Portuguese.

IT specialists

Completed activities

  • Preparation of a graphic design for the needs of the website composition using Adobe Dreamweaver and Adobe Photoshop. Performing digital photo processing and file conversion for websites.
  • Development of the structure and implementation of a website designed for the customer's requirements (eg online computer store). Testing and publishing a website.
  • Connecting to the MySQL database server used in the web application and performing on this database using the XAMPP program.Connecting web application with Microsoft SQL Server and displaying data on the website.
  • Performing the installation and configuration of Windows Server 2012, CentOS 7 and Windows 10 operating systems using the technical documentation of trainer's instructions in English.
  • Designing a logical and physical diagram of the local computer network and creating a local network in the star topology. Testing the quality of the internet connection using the traceroute, ping commands.
  • Measurement and testing of technical parameters of a personal computer (motherboard, RAM memory, hard disk, power supply, etc.) and configuration of computer and peripheral devices (printer, scanner) based on technical documentation in English.

Gained skills

  • Processing graphic elements into a website and making a website composition according to a given project.
  • Designing and publishing responsive websites in accordance with the customer's needs using current IT technologies.
  • Performing operations on a database and testing Internet applications using databases.
  • Installing and configuring Windows and Linux server operating systems using the technical documentation and trainer's instructions in English.
  • Designing, performing and testing the operation of the local computer network.
  • Test and configuration of personal computers and peripherals for specific applications using technical documentation in English.

Elektronics

Completed activities

  • Testing and performing measurements in electronic circuits, ie generators, amplifiers, power supplies: measurement of input and output voltage signals as well as current and power supply
  • Recognition and selection of digital circuits for performing logical operations, e.g. in a digital system on logic gates, to check if the car park should be open.Simulation of the operation of digital logic circuits in the Multisim program.
  • Measurement of digital parameters of combination and sequential circuit systems (gates, flip-flops, counters, registers) in TTL and CMOS technologies.
  • Installation of electronic circuits (amplifier, generator, voltage stabilizer) on the assembly board using resistors, capacitors, transistors, integrated circuits, wires. Validation of assembly without power connection and with connected power supply.
  • Designing, assembling and measuring of the power supply parameters in accordance with specific applications according to the given current-voltage characteristics.
  • Analysis of technical documentation regarding the parameters of electronic circuits, i.e. resistors, diodes, transistors, digital and analog integrated circuits and electronic devices, i.e. amplifiers, power supplies, generators, TV sets, computers and computer devices in English. The use of technical documentation, internet information and direct information from a Portuguese trainer in English for the operation and design of electronic circuits.

Gained skills

  • Making electronic measurements and selection parameters of measuring instruments, e.g.: multimeter, oscilloscope, signal generator and power supply for voltage, current and power measurement in electronic circuits (power supplies, amplifiers, generators).
  • Design, simulation of operation using PC programs and measurement of digital logic parameters, i.e. logic gates, flip-flops, counters, registers in TTL and CMOS technology.
  • Installation of analog electronic circuits on the assembly board in accordance with the given schematic diagram and checking the correctness of assembly.
  • Designing power supplies for specific applications, measuring their parameters and current-voltage characteristics. Repairing of power supplies.
  • The ability to use online sources and technical documentation on electronic systems and devices in English and to use the trainer's instructions in English during the analysis of work, design and operation of electronic systems and devices.
