Institutions

The sending institution is a Tehnical High School of Electronics from Rzeszów . The institution of acceptance is Center of Vocational Education in the Electronic Industry, Energy, Telecommunications and Information Technology - one of the largest training centers in this industry in Portugal. The practise is in cooperation with an intermediary institution Casa da Educacao.

The sending institution:

Tehnical High School of Electronics from Rzeszów

It is one of the largest high schools in the Podkarpacie region, educating technicians in computer science, electronics and automation. It educates about 1000 students. For years, he has been in the leading position in the "Perspectives" ranking. He works with countries in the European Union and abroad.

The receiving institution

CINEL

Center of Vocational Education in the Electronic Industry, Energy, Telecommunications and Information Technology - CINEL - has modern equipped facilities in Lisbon and Porto. CINEL's mission is training in the fields of electronics, energy, telecommunications and information technology, enabling the acquisition and development of professional skills. The Center cooperates closely with several institutions that use or create modern electronic and IT technologies, i.e. Samsung, Vodafone, Microsoft. CINEL has many years of experience in the implementation of international projects of the EU Leonardo da Vinci, Erasmus and Erasmus + programs under which it acts as the host and sending institution.

Intermediary institution

Casa da Educação

Casa da Educação is a closed education institution in Lisbon, operating in the field of vocational and private education. Maintains close cooperation with Portuguese and foreign institutions and companies, training teachers and students from different countries. Thus, raising their knowledge, skills and professional competences in accordance with the needs of the European labor market. The partner institution also has extensive experience in the implementation of language courses and cultural preparation for foreign youth as well as the logistics of stay at internships (accommodation, meals, local transport, etc.).
