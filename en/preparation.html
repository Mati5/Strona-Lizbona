Preparation

Before practise

A few months before practise meetings began with teachers who were preparing studentsfor an practise in Portugal. This took place at the Tehnical High School of Electronics.

Preparation included:

  • Preparation of Europass CV
  • English lessons
  • A health and safety training
  • Classes with a school teacher
  • Cultural issues
  • Organizational and logistics issues

During your stay in Portugal

During their stay in Portugal, students learned about the local customs, social life and culture. They took part in a Portuguese language course at the elementary level. In their free time, they got to know interesting places in the capital of Portugal, where they were accommodated. As part of a cultural program, one of the weekends students and their carers went on a tour of the Portugal.

  • Portuguese language course at the basic level, enabling communication in the shop, restaurant, at the station, airport (10 hours)
  • Getting to know the culture, customs and everyday life of the Portuguese
  • Visiting interesting places:
    • Sao Jorge Castle
    • Arco da Rua Augusta
    • Belem Tower
    • Jeronimos Monastery
    • Lisbon Oceanarium
    • Sanktuarium w Fatimie
    • Óbidos Village
    • Nazare Beaches
    • Cristo Rei
    • Sintra:
    • Cabo da Roca
    • Boca do Inferno
    • Cascais
