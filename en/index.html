About project

This website is about practise in Portugal, which took place from 12 Frebruary to 5 March of the year 2018r. under project PO WER PO WER VET based on the rules of the Erasmus+ “Vocational experiences – way to succes”. This practise is one of the parts of this project, which consisting of four 20-person groups of students from Technical High School of Electronics in Rzeszów in various country of Europe and the training of “Job Shadowing” for 12 teachers. On the practise in capital of Portugal – Lisbon there were 20 students of Tehnical High School of Electronics from Rzeszów, 11 IT specialists and 9 electronics with accompanyning 2 teachers.

The project's foreign partners were:

  • Center for Vocational Education of the Electronic Industry, Energy, Telecommunications and Information Technology - CINEL
  • Training institution Casa da Educação

Results of practise

On the practise in Center for Vocational Education of the Electronic Industry, Energy, Telecommunications and Information Technology - CINEL in Lisbon 20 students of Technical High School of Electronics from Rzeszów increased their technical knowledge from electronics and informatics and improved their communication skills in English. Thanks abroad practise they are able to use technical documentation in English more efficiently, deal with difficult and unusual situations, to communicate in typical situations also in Portuguese. They are more independent and responsible. They can manage their financial resources, plan their free time in an interesting way. They make new friends easier and are more open to people with a different culture, know much more about the history and everyday life of the Portuguese. They know that they can do well in science and everyday life abroad, including use various means of transport, ie metro, tram, bus, train.

An important result for each of the project participants was obtaining documents confirming the acquisition or extension of new competences:

  • Residence certificates
  • Europass Mobility documents, recognized by employees all around the Europe
  • Safety certificates
  • Certificates of finishing Portuguese course at A1 level
  • Certificate with cultural and pedagogical preparation
